Nah Son, Free Pie! Gaming

NSFPGaming.com and NSFPHax.com are both websites that I deployed for the Nah Son, Free Pie! gaming community. The main site, NSFPGaming.com, provides a portal to various other community resources such as social media presence, the online shop, community information, and associated jest sites. NSFPHax.com displays twelve of the latest videos uploaded to the "NSFPHax.com" playlist on the NSFPGaming YouTube channel using the YouTube api, and it originated from a joke within the community: while playing Counter-Strike: Global Offensive if a member pulled off an awesome play the rest of the team would spam links to where they got their hacks from — "nsfphax.com". For a while, talk of actually putting up a website which had videos of their awesome plays posted was thrown around until one day I decided I would make it happen.
